Online Donations

We understand that during the COVID-19 crisis there are many battles that need to be fought and that is why we have created this platform to provide people with materials all while trying to reduce human interaction. This is a safe place, where any interactions are anonymous. If you have any items that you don't use and would like to donate them please upload them in the forum box, and soon someone will notify you when someone would like to pick up. If you are facing financial struggles you can use this platform to browse for free items that others no longer need all while maintaining your privacy. Once you find the one you would like, click the chat button beside the image and chat with the donator.
